Yes you can have draught beer at home, or in your pub or restaurant without any contracts or red tape!!!

A good high volume draught dispenser will not cost more than the flat screen TV behind your bar

When getting quotes on a draught dispenser please make sure they include a line cleaning system ( to prevent bacteria build up in the beer lines) as easy to do as pouring a beer, and you will always serve fresh beer to your guests without foaming or any problems. A 30L or 50L keg of beer can easily last 6-8 weeks without compromising the quality of the beer.
